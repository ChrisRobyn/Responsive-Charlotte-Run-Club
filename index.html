Wednesday Night Workouts

Thursday Night Social Run

Meet us at NODA Brewing on Thursday Nights at 7pm for our weekly social run around town. After, join us for some food and fun. All paces welcome!

Welcome to Run CLT!

Run CLT is more than just a running club.. we're a community of passionate individuals who come together to embrace the joy of running and support each other in achieving our fitness goals. Based in the vibrant city of Charlotte, North Carolina, our club offers a welcoming and inclusive environment for runners of all levels and backgrounds. At Run CLT, we believe in the power of running to transform lives and build connections. Whether you're a seasoned marathoner, a casual jogger, or someone taking their first steps on the pavement, you'll find a supportive and encouraging community here.

Ready to join the movement? Sign up today and become a part of the Run CLT family!
